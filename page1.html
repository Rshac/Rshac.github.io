Who are we?

Forhisglory.me is a new outreach aimed towards encouraging women to seek God and know Him personally, as He really is. Our site is not yet finished but we plan on producing blog posts, possibly videos, and regular encouragement from God's word. Forhisglory.me is designed to give you tools to think for yourself and learn from God's word as He shows Himself to you. We trust that this will be a blessing to you. Praying we can help you dig deeper into God's beautiful love letter we call the Bible.
